Baked Chicken with Vegetables

Prep time: 30 minutes | Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 skinless chicken, cut into quarters
  • 2 white onions, sliced
  • 1/2 green pepper, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp chicken seasoning
  • Salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken with spices and salt.
  3. Arrange chicken on a tray and surround it with onions, tomatoes, carrots, zucchini, bell peppers, and potatoes.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over chicken and vegetables.
  5. Cover the tray with tinfoil and bake for 1 hour.
  6. Remove the tinfoil and let the chicken brown.

Pasta Shrimp

Prep time: 25 minutes | Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound medium pink shrimp
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup broccoli
  • 1 carrot, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 4 tbsp corn
  • 4 tbsp black olives
  • 2 mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp creamy cheese
  • 1 packet heavy whipping cream
  • 1 packet noodles
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp shrimp seasoning
  • Salt

Method

  1. Sauté shrimp in a pot with olive oil.
  2. Add cauliflower, broccoli, carrots, corn, black olives, and mushrooms. Mix well.
  3. Stir in cheese, heavy whipping cream, and seasoning. Simmer on low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Cook the noodles in boiling water, then mix them with the shrimp and vegetable mixture.

Spaghetti

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 pound minced meat
  • 1 white onion, chopped
  • 1/2 green pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp meat seasoning
  • 1 packet spaghetti
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • Salt

Method

  1. Sauté onions in a pot with a tablespoon of vegetable oil until golden brown.
  2. Add minced meat and cook with onions for 5 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, green pepper, tomato sauce, one cup of water, and seasonings. Simmer until thickened.
  4. Cook spaghetti in boiling water and mix with the meat sauce.

Grilled Lamb Chops

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lamb chops
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp rosemary
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Method

  1. Rub lamb chops with olive oil, garlic, rosemary, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Grill lamb chops on medium heat for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  4. Serve with your favorite sides.

Vegetable Stir-fry

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup broccoli
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (red, green, yellow), sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, julienned
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated

Method

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large pan.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, and zucchini. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Drizzle with soy sauce and mix well. Serve hot.
