Baked Chicken with Vegetables

Prep time: 30 minutes | Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 skinless chicken, cut-quarters
  • 2 white onions, sliced
  • 1/2 green pepper, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 1 zucchini, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp chicken seasoning
  • Salt

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Put the chicken on the tray and season with spices and salt.
  3. Arrange chicken on the tray; spread onions, tomatoes, carrots, zucchini, bell peppers, and potatoes around the chicken.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over chicken and vegetables.
  5. Cover the tray with tinfoil and bake for 1 hour.
  6. Remove tinfoil and let the chicken brown.

Pasta Shrimp

Prep time: 25 minutes | Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound medium pink shrimp
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup broccoli
  • 1 carrot, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 4 tbsp corn
  • 4 tbsp black olives
  • 2 mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp creamy cheese
  • 1 packet heavy whipping cream
  • 1 packet noodles
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp shrimp seasoning
  • Salt

Method

  1. In a pot, sauté shrimp with olive oil.
  2. Add cauliflower, broccoli, carrots, corn, black olives, and mushrooms. Mix together.
  3. Add cheese, heavy whipping cream, and seasoning. Mix and cook until combined. Simmer on low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Cook the noodles in boiling water, then add to the shrimp and vegetable mixture.

Spaghetti

Prep time: 20 minutes | Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 pound minced meat
  • 1 white onion, chopped
  • 1/2 green pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp meat seasoning
  • 1 packet spaghetti
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • Salt

Method

  1. Sauté onions in a pot until golden brown, then add a tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  2. Add minced meat and mix with onions. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, green pepper, tomato sauce, one cup of water, and seasonings. Simmer until thickened.
  4. Cook spaghetti in boiling water, then mix with the meat sauce.

Avocado Salad

Prep time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado, peeled and diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1 bunch parsley, chopped
  • 5 tbsp yogurt
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Gently toss and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Coolant Cookies

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 tea biscuit
  • 1 can Media Crema
  • 1 bag Dream Whip
  • 2 tbsp Nesquik powder
  • 2 tbsp coconut flakes
  • 4 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 4 tbsp powdered milk

Method

  1. Break the tea biscuit into a large bowl.
  2. Mix all ingredients together and pour over the biscuit.
  3. Shape into a roll, wrap in tinfoil, and freeze for 1 hour.
